millennial mancala

lets play millennial mancala!

1. *You* against *another player*
(toss the coin to start).

2. Take turns shifting all beads in one of your mini pots over, leaving one in each adjacent pot (including the big pots).

3. If you have enough beads to go around to your oponent's mini-pots, do so.

4. If your last laid bead is across from an opponets filled mini pot, you capture their mini pot and your one bead and put those beads in your big pot!

5. When/if one player has no more beads in any of their mini-pots, the other play can put all of their mini-pot beads into their big pot!

6. The player with the most beads in their big pot wins!
